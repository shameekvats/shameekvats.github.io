## Additive Manufacturing, Prototyping, and Design

Selected work in additive manufacturing, prototyping, and fabrication. The focus is on functional objects, electronics-integrated builds, art pieces, and lighting projects.

I work across concept development, CAD, slicing, print optimization, finishing, and workshop facilitation. Detailed build notes and process writeups can be added later as blog posts.
