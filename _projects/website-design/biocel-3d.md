A research-forward site for BioCel-3D, highlighting sustainable cellulose fiber development for reinforced 3D printed composites.

- **Goals:** Communicate the project mission, methods, and industrial impact while making collaboration paths clear.
- **Highlights:** Clear story around recycled cellulose electro-spinning, consortium members, publications, and news updates.
- **Stack & workflow:** Lightweight Jekyll content with structured sections for research context, updates, and partner engagement.

[Visit site](https://www.biocel3d.eu/)
