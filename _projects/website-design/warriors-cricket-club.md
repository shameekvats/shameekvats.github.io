A modern, community-focused site for Warriors Cricket Club that keeps players, fans, and sponsors aligned on fixtures, results, and news.

- **Goals:** Centralize schedules and results, spotlight squads, and provide clear sponsor placements.
- **Highlights:** Responsive layout, streamlined navigation for fixtures and stats, and sponsor callouts that feel integrated rather than intrusive.
- **Stack & workflow:** Jekyll-based templating with lightweight CSS theming for fast loads and easy content updates.

[Visit site](https://warriorscricket.eu/)
